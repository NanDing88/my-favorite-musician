Freddie Mercury

Bio

Freddie Mercury was one of best musician ever lived. He was born in Zanzibar in 1946. He began his study of music at the age of 8 in India. In 1964, Freddie and his parents fled the Zanzibar Revolution to England in 1964. Because of his unccanny passion for music, he then later formed a band called QUEEN in 1970.
